## Books

| Book | Author | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| *JavaScript: The Good Parts* | Douglas Crockford | Classic; focuses on the right patterns |
| *Eloquent JavaScript* (free online) | Marijn Haverbeke | Covers DOM, events, and async deeply |
| *DOM Enlightenment* (free online) | Cody Lindley | Focused entirely on the DOM API |
| *High Performance Browser Networking* (free online) | Ilya Grigorik | Deep dive on HTTP, CORS, and network performance |
| *Don't Make Me Think* | Steve Krug | UX thinking that informs good form and interaction design |
