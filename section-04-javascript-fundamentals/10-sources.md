## Books

| Book | Author | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| *Eloquent JavaScript* (free online) | Marijn Haverbeke | Comprehensive JS book; challenging but thorough |
| *You Don't Know JS* (free on GitHub) | Kyle Simpson | Deep dives into JS internals; excellent for understanding "why" |
| *JavaScript: The Good Parts* | Douglas Crockford | Short, opinionated classic on writing clean JS |
| *JavaScript Patterns* | Stoyan Stefanov | Design patterns and best practices |
| *Clean Code* | Robert C. Martin | Language-agnostic; great habits for any programmer |
